Ticketmaster - Mobile Apps, API & Web

'Product Intern for Mobile & API' from September 2017 - September 2018 as part of my industrial placement year.
I worked as a product manager for 28 international Ticketmaster iOS and Android apps. During the final 6 months I was made the product owner for said apps, and lead a small team of developers.

  • See what I worked on

International Mobile Apps

My projects:

  • App Store Management - I was responsible for the upload and updates of 28 iOS and Android applications on App Store Connect (iTunes Connect) and the Google Play Store. I would also manage beta testing, localisation / translation support, and creation of additional promotional material.

  • Building Analytics Dashboards - I created visual and interactive KPI dashboards for 14 markets. These were built on Google Data Studio using Google Analytics, and on the DOMO platform with data from Adobe's Omniture. The dashboards are used for internal analytics, and I presented this data monthly to company executives from all markets. I would run sessions teaching collegues how to create their own dashboards for their KPIs.

  • Reward Referrals - In an effort to increase app installs and user engagement in the Spanish market, we worked with a third party, Cinesa, to reward users with free cinema tickets when they refer a friend to install the application. Firebase was used to provide the referral functionality.

  • Help & Feedback - We built new screens to allow users to easily find help for any issues they may be having, and to collect data on the most common issues. I built a custom DOMO dashboard to keep track of the Net Promoter Score of each app version, sending alerts to the team if the score dips for our latest release.

  • API - I managed the access to the Interational API for internal users and external clients. I would be in contact with the public through email, providing API keys and support.

  • Transifex - I was the contact between the product team and translators for the application. I had to manage the Transifex platform, request and chase translations, and train translators on how to use the platform.

UK & International Website

  • Google Analytics Setup - I setup the page tracking through Google Analytics for the 'My Account' section of the Ticketmaster website redesign.
